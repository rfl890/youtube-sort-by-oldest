API Key Instructions

This application needs to get information from YouTube about the videos a channel has, and that requires a token called an API Key. It's free to generate one, and all you need is a Google Account.

Video Tutorial

Back to the website
  • Step 1: Go to https://console.cloud.google.com
  • Step 2: Sign in with your Google Account
  • Step 3: Agree to the Terms Of Service
  • Step 4: On the sidebar at the left, click on the button that says "APIs and Services"
  • Step 5: Once you are at the APIs and Services screen, click the button on the top of the page that says "Select a project"
  • Step 6: Press the "new project" button, and create a new project. The name can be whatever you want.
  • Step 7: At the APIs and Services screen, click the blue button with a plus symbol that says "Enable APIs and Services"
  • Step 8: Search for "YouTube Data API", and select "YouTube Data API v3"
  • Step 9: On the page, press the "Enable" button. You will be redirected back to the APIs and services page after this completes.
  • Step 10: On the APIs and services page, select "Credentials"
  • Step 11: There should be a button on the right side of the page that says "Create credentials", click it.
  • Step 12: In the dropdown that appears, select "API key"
  • Step 13: That's it! Copy your API key and put it into the website.
